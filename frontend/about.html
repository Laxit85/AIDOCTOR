About Us

At AI Doctor, we're here to help you better understand and care for your skin. By combining the latest in artificial intelligence with trusted dermatological knowledge, we've created a tool that makes checking your skin simple, fast, and reliable. We know how important early insights can be when it comes to skin health. That's why FaceFacts is designed to identify a wide range of skin conditions, from common issues like acne, eczema, or psoriasis to more complex concerns.

Our system provides easy-to-understand results in seconds using advanced AI technology, empowering you with the confidence and knowledge to take control of your skin health. Our mission is to make a meaningful difference in people's lives. FaceFacts bridges the gap between technology and care, offering a first step in understanding your skin and encouraging you to seek professional advice when needed. FaceFacts supports you with science-backed insights and a compassionate approach. While we're not a replacement for professional dermatologists, we're here to guide you on your journey to healthy, confident skin. Because everyone deserves to feel good in their skin.
